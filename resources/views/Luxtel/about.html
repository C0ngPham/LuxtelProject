Our Story

The Luxtel Saigon is an exquisite four star property located within the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, a short drive from the Tan Son Nhat International Airport.

Guests staying here will find themselves in a prime spot for both conducting business and touring the major historical attractions of the city, such as the Notre Dame Cathedral and nearby City Post Office, unforgettable War Remnants Museum, the Reunification Palace and Peoples Committee of Ho Chi Minh City.

There are three style of Room: Hotel Room, Luxury Room and Presidential Room
